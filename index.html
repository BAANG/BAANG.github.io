I'm a Full Stack Developer born and raised in San Diego, CA and currently living and working in San Francisco, CA.

My work ethic is my greatest strength-- and that, united with my lifelong student mentality and my child-like fascination of new and innovative technology is what keeps me from stagnating as both a professional and as a person.

I have supreme confidence in my arsenal of technical skills ranging from front-end skills in Javascript, CSS, and HTML to my ability to leverage back-end tech in Node, MySQL, MongoDB, Django, React and Python. Supplemental to my confidence is my desire to challenge myself consistently and make conscious choices to grow as a professional developer every day.
